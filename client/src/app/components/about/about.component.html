Welcome to an exciting crowdfunding platform powered by Ethereum blockchain technology. Here, a vibrant community of creators and backers come together to support innovative projects and turn dreams into reality.

As part of a course project to craft any web application of our choice, we have crafted a unique crowdfunding platform that fulfils the mandatory and optional requirements of the assessment. We have integrated various technologies to enhance the user experience, making it intuitive and seamless. Our aim is to create an engaging environment where creators can showcase their ideas and receive the support they need.
